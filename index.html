Minnal Murali

Jaison (Tovino Thomas), a tailor from the village of Kurukkanmoola obtains superpowers such as enhanced speed, high muscle power, reflexes and strength after being hit by lightning and becomes the superhero "Minnal Murali". The story follows him exploring his superpowers and how it leads to his clash between the movie's main antagonist, Selvan(Guru Somasundaram), a super-villain going by the name of "Vellidi Venkiti" , who has something to do with crows, wind and lightning.
